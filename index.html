About Us

Next Level Flooring Norfolk was established by two friends who share the same passion and commitment to the trade. With over 25 years of experience, you can rely on us to provide you with the highest quality of craftsmanship and products suited for the job in hand. We specialise in all areas of contract and industrial floor coverings. With our expertise we can provide safety flooring in a wide range of sectors including wet room adaptations. We have the solutions to suit the needs of the client, also offering full preparation work ranging from latexing through to damp proof membrane . We also provide domestic flooring varying from Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT), domestic vinyl, and carpets. With a wide selection of samples, we can offer in-home measure and quotation services, helping you choose your perfect flooring guaranteed to transform your house into a home.

Services

Supply & Fit


  • Vinyl

    Vinyl is a hard wearing, Durable and perfect for every room in your home or commerical building with the added bonus of comfort under your feet. With a large variety of patterns and styles it can add the finishing touch to your space.

  • Carpet

    Carpet is designed to provide warmth, comfort, and softness underfoot. It can be made from different natural or synthetic materials selected for their unique properties, like stain-resistance or durability. There are various types of carpet such as saxony, woven, twist, and loop pile, which all have a particular look and feel. There are so many styles and colours of carpet in our collection, it’s hard to choose! A good quality carpet can last for years - the trick is picking the right one that does what you want it to do. Thanks to our many years of flooring expertise, we can help you to decide if carpet is the right flooring for your home, and if so, which one to choose. All professional fitted by our experienced in House fitting team.

  • Luxury Vinyl Tiles

    LVT flooring, (luxury vinyl tile), is the ideal combination of everything a flooring product should be – elegantly designed, highly durable, and easy to maintain. With LVT, you can authentically mirror the look of natural materials which are Perfect for the home, but with a product that is built to withstand commercial environments.

  • Safety Flooring

    Safety flooring is specially engineered to minimise slip risk and provide safe movement in low to high traffic environments. It is often referred to as non-slip flooring. Ideal for industrial areas all the way through to low access shower room( wetroom). With a large variety of colours its the perfect solution for areas that may cause an issue from slippages.
